Analysis of NHANES Dataset

Data Mining Course Final Project

Worked with two other students on a semester-long project to perform exploratory data analysis, classification, and regression on the NHANES dataset in R. Addressed questions such as “what demographics and income levels are at higher risk of lead exposure?” by using techniques such as simple linear regression, logistic regression, gradient-boosted decision trees, and support vector machine.

Research Presentation on Asthma

Genes in Diseases and Symptoms Group Research

Worked with a group of other students to use expression profiling by array on a dataset from the NIH that contained both patients with adult-onset asthma and healthy individuals. Used STRING to identify disease pathways and focused on the proteasome and antigen processing pathways to identify genes that had significantly increased presence in the patients with asthma. Other tools used were GEO2R, KEGG Pathways, and literary review.
